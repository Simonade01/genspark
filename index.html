About the research

This project explores how AI systems can help prevent road traffic accidents. Work ranges from data-driven risk prediction and real-time alerts to prototype sensor fusion demos and policy recommendations. The goal is to build practical, scalable tools that improve safety for drivers, pedestrians, and commuters.

  • Data preprocessing & feature design
  • Real-time risk scoring
  • Sensor fusion & edge deployment
  • Policy and human factors
